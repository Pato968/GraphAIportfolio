About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity
No-Code AI Tools for Task Automation

This 8-week course explores how to harness the power of Generative AI to significantly boost workplace and research productivity. Students will learn to:

  • Automate time-consuming document, spreadsheet, and data tasks
  • Use AI tools for knowledge organization and literature reviews
  • Develop custom chatbots for projects or team use
  • Create professional and scientific visuals/presentations with AI
  • Apply knowledge in a hands-on capstone project

Includes real-time support via live workshops, help sessions, and forums.
